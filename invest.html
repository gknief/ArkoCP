Arko Capital Partners, LP | Digital Asset Hedge Fund

Arko Capital Partners, LP ("Fund") is a multi-strategy vehicle for global investors that invests in select digital assets. The Fund is predominantly driven by a discretionary strategy focused on cash-flow generative, decentralized assets. Arko Capital Partners, LP is designed to be a core holding for prudent investors seeking exposure to the cryptocurrency market. The Fund is open to accredited U.S. and non-U.S. investors seeking to invest $100,000 or more in digital assets or blockchain-enabled protocols.
